Face Prediction Project

Fully developed by Hunter Mitchell



Both models are trained with over 3000 images of faces from the imdb-wiki dataset. One is a simple classification model and the other is a bit more complicated regression model. They each use the widely popular convolutional neural network DenseNet-201. The gender prediction model has an accuracy of about 92%, whereas the age prediction model has a mean absolute error of less than 8. Both models work better when you are closer to the camera. Test out what works and what doesn't and feel free to let me know! I look forward to implementing more models to this interface very soon!
